## what-it-does
A trivia game ability for OpenHome speakers. The Personality asks trivia questions aloud and tracks user answers.

## how-to-build
Pulls questions from a trivia API, speaks them via the Personality TTS, and listens for voice answers. Keeps score across rounds.
