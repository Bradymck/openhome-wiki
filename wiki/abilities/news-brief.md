## what-it-does
The News Brief ability is designed to capture and store moments of curiosity that arise during conversations. It operates silently in the background, allowing users to talk naturally without specific trigger words. When a user wants to revisit their curiosities, they can ask, "What am I curious about?" and receive a list of their saved queries, organized by type and status.

## how-to-build
Building the News Brief ability involves implementing a mechanism that captures user inquiries without interrupting the flow of conversation. The ability must consist of logic to:
- Ensure unique topics are captured to avoid duplicates.
- Organize curiosities by categories such as how, why, and what questions.
- Retain queries across multiple sessions.
- Notify users in real-time if new curiosities are added (optional).
- Distinguish between answered and pending questions.
