News Brief is an Ability plugin designed to provide users with concise updates on current events and news topics, enhancing the informational capabilities of your AI Personality.

To build this Ability, developers can utilize the OpenHome SDK. The Ability will call various news APIs to fetch and summarize the latest news articles. It should include a trigger hotword and execute custom Python logic to parse the news data and present it to the user in a conversational manner.
