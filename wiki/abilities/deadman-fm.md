## what-it-does
deadman.fm (💀.fm) is a decentralized pirate radio ability. Stations broadcast forever as long as the operator sends a heartbeat every 7 days. Miss the heartbeat and the station goes dark. Built on Hypercore P2P + Base (V4 contracts).

## how-to-build
Built on ChaCha20-Poly1305 encrypted streams over Hypercore. Operators register stations via on-chain contracts on Base. The OpenHome ability plays any 💀.fm station by name or address.
