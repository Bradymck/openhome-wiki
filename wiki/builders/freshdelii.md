### who-they-are
FreshDelii is a community builder engaged in developing capabilities for OpenHome, a voice AI platform. They focus on creating conversational AI agents called Personalities that can be enhanced with modular voice plugins known as Abilities.

### what-they-built
FreshDelii has contributed to the development of Local Abilities, which operate directly on DevKit hardware. These Abilities allow for features like direct hardware access, running shell commands, and managing device-level data, enhancing the functionality of voice AI agents within the OpenHome ecosystem.
