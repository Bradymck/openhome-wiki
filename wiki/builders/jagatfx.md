## who-they-are
Jagatfx is an active contributor within the OpenHome community, focusing on building abilities for conversational AI agents. They aim to enhance interactions by creating functionalities that leverage voice and AI technologies effectively.

## what-they-built
Jagatfx has developed a passive ability called **Social Memory** that integrates with OpenHome. This ability tracks mentions of people in conversations without requiring explicit commands from the user. It intelligently captures context during conversations and nudges users for follow-ups based on the collected data. The development process involved a two-phase detection approach for optimal performance. A pull request detailing the implementation of this ability can be found [here](https://github.com/openhome-dev/abilities/pull/264).
