### definition
Abilities-as-apps refer to modular voice plugins within the OpenHome platform that enhance conversational AI agents (referred to as Personalities) by providing them with new skills or functionalities.

### why-it-matters
This concept is crucial to OpenHome because it facilitates the expansion of AI capabilities in voice interactions, allowing users to customize and evolve their AI agents to meet specific needs and preferences. The flexibility of abilities-as-apps encourages innovation among developers and enhances the overall user experience.

### how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome platform, abilities-as-apps are implemented as plug-ins that can be integrated into Personalities. These abilities are triggered by specific hotwords during voice conversations and can perform a variety of tasks, such as calling APIs, playing music, running quizzes, or controlling smart home devices. Developers can create and share their own custom abilities through community platforms like GitHub.
