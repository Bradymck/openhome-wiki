## definition
Abilities-as-apps refers to modular voice plugins within the OpenHome platform that enhanced the capabilities of AI agents, known as Personalities. These abilities can perform various tasks, such as calling APIs, playing music, controlling smart home devices, and running interactive quizzes.

## why-it-matters
This concept is essential to OpenHome as it enables developers and users to extend the functionality of their AI agents dynamically, fostering a diverse ecosystem of applications. The modular nature of abilities encourages innovation and allows for personalized user experiences, making technology interactions more intuitive and user-friendly.

## how-it-manifests
Abilities are integrated into the OpenHome platform, where they are triggered by specific hotwords during voice interactions. Developers can build their own abilities or utilize community-created ones, allowing for rapid prototyping and testing of new functionalities. Additionally, these abilities can be classified as either system abilities, accessible by any agent, or agent-specific abilities, reserved for individual agents.
