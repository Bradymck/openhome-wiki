## definition
The concept of "abilities-as-apps" refers to the way in which various functionalities and features within the OpenHome platform are designed similarly to applications, allowing users to easily access and utilize them independently. "Abilities-as-apps" is significant for OpenHome because it enhances user engagement and customization. Users can select, install, and manage abilities as they would with apps on a smartphone, promoting a personalized experience that caters to individual preferences and needs within the home ecosystem.

## how-it-manifests
This concept manifests within the OpenHome platform through a dedicated marketplace or interface where users can browse, install, and configure abilities as standalone applications. These abilities could range from home automation features to security functionalities, all available for users to manage at their convenience.
