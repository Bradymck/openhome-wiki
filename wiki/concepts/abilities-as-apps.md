## definition
Abilities-as-apps refers to a design approach in which functional capabilities or skills (abilities) are structured and presented similar to applications, enabling modular use and easy access within a platform.

## why-it-matters
This concept is significant to OpenHome as it fosters user customization and personalization. By treating abilities as discrete apps, users can choose and integrate the specific functionalities that enhance their interactions and experiences within the platform.

## how-it-manifests
Abilities-as-apps can be observed throughout OpenHome where users can select from a variety of pre-built capabilities (e.g., voice recognition, environmental controls) that act as standalone applications, facilitating an intuitive and flexible home management system.
