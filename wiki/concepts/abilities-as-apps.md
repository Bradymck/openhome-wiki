### definition
Abilities-as-apps refers to the modular voice plugins within the OpenHome platform that enhance conversational AI agents, known as Personalities, by adding new skills and functionalities.

### why-it-matters
This concept is critical to the OpenHome ecosystem as it allows users and developers to tailor AI interactions to specific needs, making technology more accessible and customizable. The ability to add various skills empowers users to create highly versatile AI agents that can perform a multitude of tasks through voice commands.

### how-it-manifests
Abilities manifest in the OpenHome platform as distinct plugins that can be integrated into Personalities. These abilities can handle various tasks such as calling APIs, playing music, managing smart home devices, running quizzes, and more. They are triggered by specific hotwords during interactive voice conversations, enabling a seamless user experience.
