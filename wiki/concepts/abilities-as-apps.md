### definition
Abilities-as-apps in OpenHome refer to modular voice plugins that enhance the functionality of conversational AI agents, known as Personalities. These abilities can perform various tasks such as calling APIs, playing music, and controlling smart devices.

### why-it-matters
This concept is crucial to the OpenHome platform as it allows developers to create customizable and extendable AI experiences. It promotes a collaborative ecosystem where users can contribute their own abilities and interact with the broader community, thereby enhancing the overall capabilities of the platform.

### how-it-manifests
Abilities manifest in the OpenHome platform as plugins that can be added to Personalities. They are triggered by specific hotwords during voice conversations and consist of custom Python logic that defines their behavior. Users can browse, fork, and submit their own abilities through community resources like GitHub.
