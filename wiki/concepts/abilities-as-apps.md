## definition
Abilities as apps refer to modular voice plugins within the OpenHome platform that extend the functionality of conversational AI agents, known as Personalities. Each Ability adds a new skill, such as controlling smart devices, playing music, or running quizzes, making the AI interaction more versatile.

## why-it-matters
This concept is central to the OpenHome ecosystem because it enables users to customize and enhance their AI interactions by integrating various functionalities seamlessly. By allowing developers to create and share Abilities, OpenHome fosters community contribution and innovation, encouraging a diverse range of applications that cater to different user needs.

## how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome platform, Abilities are implemented as plugins that are triggered during voice conversations through hotwords. Developers can create new Abilities using custom Python logic, allowing for a wide range of potential applications. Users can browse and explore community-created Abilities, contributing to a growing library of functionalities that enhance the capabilities of their AI agents.
