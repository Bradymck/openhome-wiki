## definition
Abilities-as-apps refers to the modular plugins integrated into the OpenHome platform that extend the functionality of AI agents, enabling them to perform a variety of tasks and skills through voice interactions.

## why-it-matters
This concept is crucial for OpenHome as it allows users to create highly versatile AI agents, known as Personalities, that can engage in complex interactions. By enabling these abilities, OpenHome fosters an environment where users can customize their AI experiences, thereby enhancing user engagement and practical utility.

## how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome platform, Abilities are implemented as plugins that can be attached to Personalities. Each Ability can be triggered by specific voice commands or hotwords during conversations, allowing for seamless interaction. Developers can create their own Abilities using custom Python logic and contribute them to the OpenHome ecosystem, allowing for a community-driven expansion of functionalities.
