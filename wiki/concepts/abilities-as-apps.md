### definition
Abilities-as-apps are modular voice plugins that enhance a conversational AI agent, known as a Personality, by adding new skills or functionalities. These abilities can execute various tasks such as retrieving information, playing music, or controlling smart home devices based on user interactions.

### why-it-matters
This concept is central to OpenHome as it empowers developers to extend the capabilities of their AI agents dynamically. By treating abilities as individual apps, OpenHome fosters a flexible ecosystem where developers can share and collaborate on different skills, ultimately enhancing user experience and engagement.

### how-it-manifests
Abilities are integrated into the OpenHome platform and can be triggered through specific hotwords during voice conversations. Developers can create or prototype new abilities using OpenHome's dashboard or GitHub repository, allowing for an expansive variety of functionalities tailored to user needs.
