## definition
Abilities-as-apps refer to modular voice plugins within the OpenHome platform that enhance AI agents (known as Personalities) by adding specific skills or functionalities. These abilities can be triggered during voice interactions, enabling the agent to perform tasks like controlling devices, fetching information, or engaging users in activities like quizzes.

## why-it-matters
This concept is critical to OpenHome as it allows developers and users to customize and expand the capabilities of AI agents, making them more versatile and functional. By leveraging abilities-as-apps, users can create personalized interactions that cater to their unique needs and preferences, thereby enhancing user engagement and satisfaction.

## how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome platform, abilities are developed and integrated as plugins that can be installed and activated for different Personalities. Abilities are triggered through specific voice commands (hotwords), allowing users to interact seamlessly with their AI agents. Developers can create, share, and discover community-built abilities via the OpenHome ecosystem.
