### definition
The "abilities-as-apps" concept refers to the idea of treating individual abilities or functions as discrete applications within a platform, making them easily accessible and manageable for users.

### why-it-matters
This concept is significant to OpenHome as it enhances user experience by allowing a modular approach to functionalities. Users can customize their home management systems by selecting and integrating only the abilities they need, thereby improving efficiency and personal relevance.

### how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome platform, "abilities-as-apps" can be seen in the way users can download, install, and manage various functionalities designed to control different aspects of home automation. Each ability can function independently or in conjunction with others, providing a flexible and user-friendly environment.
