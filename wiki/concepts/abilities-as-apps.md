### definition
The "abilities-as-apps" concept in OpenHome refers to modular voice plugins called Abilities that can be added to AI agents, enabling them to perform a variety of tasks and functions, similar to how applications extend the capability of an operating system.

### why-it-matters
This concept is crucial for OpenHome as it allows developers to create personalized, interactive experiences for users by integrating diverse functionalities into conversational AI agents. It enhances the platform's flexibility, making it easier to adapt to users' needs.

### how-it-manifests
Abilities manifest in the OpenHome platform as interactive plugins that are triggered through voice commands during conversations. These plugins can perform actions like playing music, managing smart home devices, calling APIs, and running interactive quizzes. Developers can create and share their own Abilities through the OpenHome community, enriching the ecosystem with new functionalities.
