### definition
Abilities-as-apps refer to the modular voice plugins within the OpenHome platform that enhance the functionality of AI agents, known as Personalities. These plugins add diverse skills, such as calling APIs, playing music, and controlling smart home devices, to the AI agents.

### why-it-matters
This concept is crucial for OpenHome as it empowers developers to create highly customizable and interactive voice experiences. By allowing the integration of various abilities, the platform enables users to tailor their AI agents to meet specific needs and preferences, ultimately enhancing user engagement and satisfaction.

### how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome platform, abilities are triggered during voice conversations through specific hotwords. They allow Personalities to perform actions dynamically based on user input. Developers can create new abilities and contribute to the community repository, promoting a collaborative environment for innovation and expansion of the platform's capabilities.
