### definition
Abilities-as-apps refers to the modular voice plugins within the OpenHome platform that extend the functionality of conversational AI agents, known as Personalities. Each Ability grants new skills, enabling the agent to perform various tasks such as calling APIs, playing music, or controlling smart home devices.

### why-it-matters
This concept is crucial to OpenHome because it promotes a flexible and customizable approach for developers to enhance AI interactions. By utilizing Abilities, developers can tailor their agents to meet specific user needs, fostering innovation and improving user experiences in voice-powered applications.

### how-it-manifests
The concept of Abilities-as-apps is present throughout the OpenHome platform, evidenced by its architecture that allows developers to create and add these modular plugins. Abilities can be triggered by specific hotwords during a voice conversation, and they run custom logic coded in Python, allowing for a wide range of actions and interactions.
