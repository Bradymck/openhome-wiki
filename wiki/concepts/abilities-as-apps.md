## definition
Abilities are the OpenHome equivalent of apps — but they run locally, respond to voice, and extend a Personality rather than sitting on a screen. They are Python plugins with a clear API surface.

## why-it-matters
The Ability model is what makes OpenHome extensible. Instead of a closed assistant with fixed features, every user gets a speaker they can customize with community-built capabilities.

## how-it-manifests
The Marketplace, the CapabilityWorker SDK, the Live Editor, and the Grant Program all exist to support the Ability ecosystem.
