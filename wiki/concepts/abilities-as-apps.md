### definition
Abilities as apps refer to modular voice plugins that enhance the functionality of AI agents, called Personalities, within the OpenHome platform. These plugins provide new skills and features, allowing users to customize their voice interactions.

### why-it-matters
This concept is crucial for OpenHome because it allows flexibility and extensibility in the capabilities of AI agents. By enabling users to create and share Abilities, OpenHome promotes a collaborative environment for innovation, making it easier to incorporate diverse functionalities to meet user needs.

### how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome platform, Abilities manifest as plugins that can perform various tasks, such as calling APIs, playing music, running quizzes, and controlling smart devices. Users can trigger these Abilities using specific hotwords during voice conversations, enabling dynamic interactions tailored to each user's preferences.
