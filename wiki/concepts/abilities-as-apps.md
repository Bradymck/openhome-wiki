### definition
The concept of "abilities-as-apps" refers to the idea of packaging certain functionalities or capabilities as discrete applications within a platform, allowing users to easily access and utilize various features to enhance their experience.

### why-it-matters
This concept is significant to OpenHome as it enables a modular approach to functionality, allowing for customization and personalization of user experiences. By treating abilities as standalone apps, users can choose and configure functionalities that best suit their needs, improving user satisfaction and engagement with the platform.

### how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome platform, abilities-as-apps may manifest in the form of downloadable applications or modules that users can install, activate, or deactivate based on their preferences. This flexibility allows users to curate their environment according to their specific requirements, fostering a more adaptable and user-friendly interface.
