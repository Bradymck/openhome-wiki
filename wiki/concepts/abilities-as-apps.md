### definition
Abilities-as-apps refers to a modular system within the OpenHome platform that allows users to enhance their voice AI agents, known as Personalities, by integrating various skills through plugins called Abilities. These Abilities enable functionalities such as playing music, controlling smart devices, or retrieving information via APIs.

### why-it-matters
This concept is significant to OpenHome as it empowers developers and users to create diverse and personalized voice interactions. By allowing the addition of Abilities, OpenHome can cater to a vast array of scenarios and user needs, thereby enhancing the overall user experience and engagement with the platform.

### how-it-manifests
Abilities-as-apps manifest in OpenHome through the integration of plugins that can be added to Personalities. Users can create or install Abilities, which are then triggered by voice commands during interactions. These Abilities can function as either system-wide capabilities or be specified to work with particular agents. The community plays a crucial role in this by contributing new Abilities and sharing their creations, enhancing the platform's capabilities.
