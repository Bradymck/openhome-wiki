### definition
Abilities as apps refer to the concept of modular functionalities that can be instantiated and executed independently within a digital platform, akin to applications. Each ability represents a specific task or feature, providing users with personalized solutions and enhancing their interaction with the platform.

### why-it-matters
This concept is significant to OpenHome as it enables a flexible, user-driven experience. By allowing users to customize and optimize their interactions through various abilities, the platform can cater to diverse needs and preferences, fostering greater engagement and satisfaction.

### how-it-manifests
On the OpenHome platform, abilities as apps are integrated to provide users with a range of functionalities that can be utilized as needed. This manifests in user interfaces that allow the selection and activation of these abilities, creating a tailored experience based on individual user requirements.
