## definition
Abilities as Apps are modular voice plugins in the OpenHome platform that extend the functionality of AI agents, referred to as Personalities. These plugins add new skills, such as calling APIs, playing music, running quizzes, and controlling smart home devices.

## why-it-matters
This concept is crucial to OpenHome as it allows for a flexible and dynamic way to enhance AI agents. By treating abilities as separate apps, developers can innovate and iterate rapidly, leading to a diverse ecosystem of skills that improve user interaction and engagement.

## how-it-manifests
Abilities manifest in the OpenHome platform by being triggered during voice conversations through specific hotwords. Each Ability runs custom Python logic, making it possible to build interactive and responsive experiences for users. Additionally, the integration of community-created Abilities encourages collaboration and expansion of the platform's functionalities.
