## definition
Abilities-as-apps refers to modular voice plugins within OpenHome that enhance the capabilities of conversational AI agents, known as Personalities. These plugins allow agents to perform various tasks, such as calling APIs, playing music, and controlling smart home devices, thus expanding their functionality.

## why-it-matters
This concept is crucial to OpenHome as it empowers developers to create versatile and unique user experiences. By enabling the integration of diverse abilities, OpenHome fosters innovation and personalization in voice interactions, making technology more accessible and relevant to individual users' needs.

## how-it-manifests
Abilities-as-apps are implemented in the OpenHome platform as plugins that can be added to Personalities. Developers can create their own abilities or explore existing ones through community contributions. These abilities are typically activated by trigger words during voice conversations, allowing users to seamlessly engage with their AI agents.
