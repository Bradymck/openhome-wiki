### definition
Abilities-as-apps refer to modular voice plugins within the OpenHome platform that enhance conversational AI agents, known as Personalities, by adding new skills. These abilities can perform various functions, such as calling APIs, playing music, or controlling smart home devices, and are triggered during voice interactions.

### why-it-matters
This concept is essential to OpenHome as it allows developers to create and extend AI functionalities easily, promoting innovation and customization. By treating abilities as standalone apps, OpenHome encourages the development of countless features that can cater to diverse user needs, ultimately enhancing user experience and engagement.

### how-it-manifests
Abilities are integrated within the OpenHome ecosystem, where developers can create, share, and enhance these plugins through community engagement. Users can explore a library of existing abilities, fork them for their projects, and develop new functionalities, fostering an active development community around OpenHome. The ease of adding abilities enhances the overall capabilities of Personalities, making them more versatile and useful in real-world applications.
