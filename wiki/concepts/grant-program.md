## definition
A grant program is a structured initiative that provides funding or resources to support specific projects, ideas, or research that align with particular goals or objectives.

## why-it-matters
Grant programs are crucial for fostering innovation, supporting community development, and enabling individuals and organizations to pursue initiatives that may not be financially viable otherwise. In the context of OpenHome, grant programs can empower developers, researchers, and community members to create new applications or enhancements that contribute to the platform's ecosystem.

## how-it-manifests
In OpenHome, grant programs can manifest as funding opportunities for developers to build applications, improve features, or engage in research related to open-source technologies and community-driven approaches. These programs may include specific criteria for eligibility, application processes, and follow-up assessments to ensure the funds lead to impactful results.
