### definition
A grant program is a structured initiative that provides funding or resources to individuals or organizations to pursue specific projects or research endeavors beneficial to OpenHome's mission.

### why-it-matters
Grant programs are essential for fostering innovation, creativity, and collaboration within the OpenHome ecosystem. They support developers, researchers, and community members in creating new applications, tools, or research that can contribute to the overall growth and sustainability of the platform.

### how-it-manifests
Grant programs manifest as formal calls for proposals where potential grant recipients apply for funding. These may be publicized through OpenHome's communication channels or community events, encouraging diverse participation from the community. Successful applications typically align with OpenHome's goals and demonstrate potential impact on the platform's development.
