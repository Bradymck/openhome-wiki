### definition
A grant program is a funding initiative that provides financial resources to individuals, organizations, or projects to achieve specific goals or support activities that align with the overarching mission of OpenHome.

### why-it-matters
Grant programs are crucial for fostering innovation, supporting community initiatives, and ensuring that resources are allocated to projects that enhance the OpenHome ecosystem. They empower creators and developers to explore new ideas and bring valuable solutions to life.

### how-it-manifests
In OpenHome, grant programs manifest as structured opportunities for applicants to receive funding for projects that align with the goals of the platform. This includes support for developing new features, research, and community-building activities that advance the shared vision of OpenHome.
