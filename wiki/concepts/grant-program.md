## definition
The Grant Program is an initiative within OpenHome to support and incentivize developers by providing funding and resources for innovative projects. It aims to foster creativity and collaboration in enhancing the OpenHome platform.

## why-it-matters
The Grant Program is significant to OpenHome as it not only encourages community participation but also accelerates the development of new applications and features for the platform. By offering financial support and resources, OpenHome can leverage the collective talent of its user base to drive technological advancements.

## how-it-manifests
The Grant Program manifests through opportunities for developers to receive funding in the form of physical devkits and additional budget based on their contributions and project feedback. Participants are encouraged to prototype and share their ideas, which helps build a vibrant ecosystem around the OpenHome platform.
