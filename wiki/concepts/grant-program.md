## definition
A grant program is an initiative that allocates funds or resources to support projects, research, or developments that align with specific goals or criteria.

## why-it-matters
Grant programs are essential for fostering innovation and supporting initiatives within the OpenHome community. They provide crucial financial backing that can empower developers, researchers, and entrepreneurs to create impactful solutions and contribute to the overall growth and success of the platform.

## how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome ecosystem, grant programs may be implemented through open calls for proposals, where individuals or teams can apply for funding to develop new features, conduct research, or enhance existing functionalities. These grants can help accelerate the development of applications and technologies related to the platform.
