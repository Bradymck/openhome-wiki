### definition
The Grant Program is an initiative by OpenHome that provides financial support and resources to developers and teams who contribute to the OpenHome platform. This program incentivizes the creation of innovative applications and functionalities using the OpenHome framework.

### why-it-matters
The Grant Program is crucial for the growth of the OpenHome ecosystem as it fosters community engagement and encourages the development of diverse applications. By supporting developers, OpenHome enhances its platform's capabilities and creates a richer user experience.

### how-it-manifests
The program manifests through the distribution of physical development kits (DevKits) to top contributors. Additionally, recipients of the grants are promoted within the community, and their projects may receive extra funding. This initiative is designed to stimulate creativity and experimentation within the OpenHome community.
