## definition
The Grant Program is an initiative within the OpenHome ecosystem that supports developers and contributors by providing funding and resources to enhance their projects and contributions.

## why-it-matters
The Grant Program is significant to OpenHome as it incentivizes innovation and encourages community engagement. By financially supporting top contributors, it fosters a collaborative environment where users can share ideas and develop new capabilities within the platform.

## how-it-manifests
The Grant Program manifests through the distribution of physical development kits (DevKits) to top contributors, along with supplementary budget allocations for their projects. Recipients of these grants can receive promotional support for their developments and are encouraged to actively participate by sharing their work and feedback.
