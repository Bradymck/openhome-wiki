## definition
A grant program is a structured initiative designed to provide financial assistance or resources to individuals or developers who contribute positively to the OpenHome ecosystem. This program encourages innovation and participation within the community.

## why-it-matters
The grant program is integral to fostering developer engagement and driving the growth of OpenHome. By incentivizing contributions and offering support, it helps accelerate the development of new features, plugins, and applications that enhance the platform.

## how-it-manifests
The grant program typically manifests through the distribution of physical development kits (DevKits) to top contributors, allowing them to prototype and build new abilities for the OpenHome platform. Recipients of grants may also receive financial support for their projects, additional resources, and promotional opportunities to showcase their work within the OpenHome community.
