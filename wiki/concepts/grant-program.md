## definition
The Grant Program is an initiative by OpenHome aimed at encouraging contributions from developers by providing support, financial resources, and rewards for innovative projects built on the OpenHome platform.

## why-it-matters
This program fosters a community of creators and innovators who contribute to the growth and enhancement of the OpenHome ecosystem. By rewarding top contributions, OpenHome can accelerate the development of new features and capabilities that benefit all users.

## how-it-manifests
The Grant Program is integrated into the development environment of OpenHome through initiatives such as the distribution of physical development kits (devkits) to contributors. Developers who actively participate by prototyping new abilities or providing feedback can receive these devkits and additional funding to support their projects.
