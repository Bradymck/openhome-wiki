## definition
Voice-first refers to the design and interaction paradigm that prioritizes voice as the primary mode of communication with technology. This concept emphasizes using natural language processing to facilitate user engagement through spoken commands and responses, making technology more accessible and intuitive.

## why-it-matters
In OpenHome, voice-first is significant because it aligns with the goal of creating more natural and user-friendly interfaces. As homes become increasingly connected through smart technology, enabling voice interaction allows for seamless control and fosters an inclusive environment for users of varying technological proficiency.

## how-it-manifests
Within the platform, voice-first technology is integrated into various features, allowing users to control smart devices, access information, and manage tasks through voice commands. This capability enhances user experience by providing hands-free convenience and enabling multitasking within the home environment.
