## definition
Voice-first means the primary interaction mode is spoken language — no screen required, no touch required. The AI interprets intent from speech and responds in kind.

## why-it-matters
Voice-first unlocks ambient computing: AI that responds without demanding attention, fits naturally into daily life, and requires no UI expertise to use.

## how-it-manifests
Every OpenHome speaker interaction, every Ability, every Personality is designed around voice as the primary interface.
