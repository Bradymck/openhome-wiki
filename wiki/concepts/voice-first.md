## definition
Voice-first refers to the paradigm in which voice recognition and voice interaction are the primary modes of communication and control between users and technology, prioritizing voice over traditional input methods like touch or keyboard.

## why-it-matters
Voice-first technology is significant for OpenHome as it enables hands-free interactions, making the platform more accessible and user-friendly. It allows users to engage with their homes and connected devices in a more natural and intuitive manner, enhancing the overall user experience.

## how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome platform, voice-first capabilities may appear in features such as voice commands for controlling smart devices, setting reminders, playing music, or retrieving information. This integration allows users to manage their home environment seamlessly and efficiently through conversational interfaces.
