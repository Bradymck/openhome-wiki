## definition
Voice-first refers to the design and interaction paradigm where voice is the primary medium for user interface and communication with technology. This approach prioritizes conversational interactions, allowing users to control devices and access information through spoken commands.

## why-it-matters
Voice-first technology is central to OpenHome's mission of making AI-powered applications accessible and enhancing human-technology interactions. By focusing on voice, OpenHome aims to create intuitive, immersive experiences that empower users and transform how they engage with their devices.

## how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome platform, voice-first capabilities are implemented through Personalities (AI agents) that users can create and customize. These Personalities interact with users using natural language, responding to voice commands and questions. The platform also supports Abilities, which are voice-activated plugins that extend the functionality of Personalities, enabling them to perform a variety of tasks from playing music to controlling smart home devices.
