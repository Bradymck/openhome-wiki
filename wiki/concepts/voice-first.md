### definition
Voice-first refers to a design and interaction paradigm where voice commands and speech recognition are the primary means of accessing and controlling technology.

### why-it-matters
In the context of OpenHome, adopting a voice-first approach enhances user experience by allowing intuitive and hands-free interaction with the platform. This is particularly relevant as more users embrace smart home technologies, where voice commands can improve accessibility and convenience.

### how-it-manifests
Voice-first technology is integrated into various features of the OpenHome platform. Users can control smart devices, manage settings, and access information through voice commands, which simplifies the process and makes it more user-friendly.
