### definition
Voice-first refers to a paradigm where voice interactions with devices and applications are prioritized over traditional visual interfaces. This approach enables users to engage with technology through spoken language, making the experience more natural and intuitive.

### why-it-matters
Voice-first technology is crucial for enhancing user experience within OpenHome, as it allows for seamless interaction and accessibility, catering to users who may prefer or require hands-free control. It promotes inclusivity, making technology more accessible to individuals with varying abilities.

### how-it-manifests
Within the OpenHome platform, voice-first capabilities can be found in various features such as voice-activated commands for home automation, personal assistant functions, and customer support interactions. This functionality enables users to manage their home environment and access information effortlessly.
