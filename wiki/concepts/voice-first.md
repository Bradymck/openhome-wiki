## definition
Voice-first refers to a technology paradigm where voice is the primary medium for interacting with devices and applications. This approach emphasizes natural language processing, allowing users to communicate with technology using spoken language rather than traditional input methods like keyboards or touch screens.

## why-it-matters
Voice-first technology is central to OpenHome's mission to revolutionize how users interact with their devices. It aims to provide more intuitive and human-like interactions, making AI-powered voice applications accessible and enhancing everyday life.

## how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome platform, voice-first interactions are embodied through the creation of Personalities, which are AI agents that users can customize. These agents leverage voice input to trigger Abilities, which are modular voice plugins. The use of hotwords during conversations allows for seamless and effective interaction with the technology, showcasing the platform's voice-first capabilities.
