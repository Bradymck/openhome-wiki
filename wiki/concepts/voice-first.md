## definition
Voice-first is a design approach that prioritizes voice interactions as the primary method of communication with technology, focusing on natural language processing and conversational interfaces.

## why-it-matters
This concept is crucial to OpenHome as it enhances accessibility, allowing users to interact with the platform through speech. It aligns with the trend toward more intuitive user interfaces, making technology more approachable for individuals who may find traditional input methods challenging.

## how-it-manifests
In OpenHome, voice-first capabilities are integrated into various functionalities, enabling users to perform tasks, receive information, and control systems through voice commands. This includes features like voice-activated assistants and interfaces that respond to spoken inquiries, significantly improving user experience and engagement.
