## definition
Voice-first refers to a design paradigm where interactions with technology are primarily conducted through voice commands, prioritizing verbal communication over traditional graphical interfaces. This approach aims to create more natural and human-like interactions between users and devices.

## why-it-matters
Voice-first technology is central to OpenHome's mission of revolutionizing how users interact with their devices. By making AI-powered, voice-first applications accessible, OpenHome allows for immersive and intuitive interactions that empower users to manage their smart environments seamlessly through conversation.

## how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome platform, the voice-first concept is manifested through Personalities, which are AI agents capable of engaging in conversations. These agents can perform tasks, respond to commands, and control smart home devices using voice inputs. Additionally, the platform supports Abilities that enhance these interactions, enabling users to trigger functionalities through specific hotwords during voice conversations.
