## definition
Voice-first refers to the design and interaction paradigm that prioritizes voice as the primary medium for communication with technology, allowing users to engage with devices through spoken commands and conversations. 

## why-it-matters
This concept is crucial for OpenHome as it aligns with the platform's mission to enhance human-device interaction, making technology more accessible and intuitive through natural voice communication. It facilitates the development of more engaging and immersive applications that leverage conversational AI.

## how-it-manifests
In OpenHome, voice-first technology is evident in the functionality of Personalities—AI agents designed to converse with users. These agents utilize voice recognition and natural language processing to understand and respond to voice commands. Additionally, Abilities—modular plugins—enhance these Personalities, allowing for a range of interactive features triggered by voice interactions.
