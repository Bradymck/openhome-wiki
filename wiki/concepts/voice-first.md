## definition
Voice-first refers to an interactive approach where voice commands are the primary method of interaction with technology. This paradigm shift emphasizes conversational interfaces that allow users to communicate with devices using natural language, rather than traditional input methods like keyboards or touchscreens.

## why-it-matters
Voice-first technology is at the core of OpenHome's mission to revolutionize human-device interactions. By prioritizing voice as the primary means of engagement, OpenHome aims to make technology more accessible, intuitive, and user-friendly. This shift not only enhances user experiences but also empowers diverse demographics to leverage AI-powered functionalities without needing technical proficiency.

## how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome platform, the voice-first approach manifests through the development of Personalities—AI agents designed to engage users in natural conversations. Users can interact with these agents by issuing voice commands, which can trigger various Abilities. The platform is built to recognize hotwords and understand context, facilitating seamless interactions that mimic human conversation.
