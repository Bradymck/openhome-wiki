## definition
Voice-first is an approach to digital interaction that prioritizes voice commands and conversations as the primary means of user engagement with technology, rather than traditional input methods like keyboards or touchscreens.

## why-it-matters
The voice-first concept is crucial to OpenHome as it enhances accessibility and user experience. By leveraging voice interactions, OpenHome can create a more intuitive and seamless environment for users, aligning with modern communication trends and accommodating diverse user capabilities.

## how-it-manifests
Within the OpenHome platform, voice-first functionality is integrated into various applications and tools. Users can interact with services using natural language commands, receive feedback, and access information effortlessly. This capability may be reflected in smart home automation features, virtual assistants, and customer support systems that use voice recognition technologies.
