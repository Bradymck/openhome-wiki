## definition
Spatial intelligence refers to the ability to visualize and manipulate objects in a three-dimensional space. It involves understanding and interacting with the environment and its spatial relationships, enabling individuals to solve problems posed by their surroundings.

## why-it-matters
Spatial intelligence is crucial for the OpenHome platform as it enhances user interaction and experience. By leveraging spatial awareness, OpenHome can provide more intuitive navigation and management of living spaces, allowing users to interact with their environment more effectively.

## how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome platform, spatial intelligence manifests through features such as 3D visualizations of user environments, spatial mapping for device placement, and augmented reality overlays that facilitate interaction with home automation features. These functionalities enable users to have a better understanding of their spaces and enhance their operational efficiency.
