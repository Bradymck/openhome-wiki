## definition
Spatial intelligence is the ability to visualize and manipulate spatial relationships in the environment. This includes recognizing patterns, understanding how objects relate to one another in space, and thinking in three dimensions.

## why-it-matters
In the context of OpenHome, spatial intelligence is crucial as it enhances user interaction with digital environments. It allows for more intuitive navigation and interaction, improving user experience. This intelligence can inform design choices that make the platform more accessible and engaging.

## how-it-manifests
Spatial intelligence manifests within OpenHome in various ways, such as through user interface design that prioritizes spatial relationships. This can include layout decisions, navigation systems, and the integration of augmented reality features that leverage spatial reasoning for enhanced user engagement. The concept also plays a role in how users can visualize their homes and the arrangement of digital elements within their personal space.
