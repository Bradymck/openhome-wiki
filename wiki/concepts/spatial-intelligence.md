## definition
Spatial intelligence refers to the ability to visualize and manipulate spatial relationships in one's mind. It involves skills such as understanding maps, diagrams, and three-dimensional objects, enabling individuals to navigate and interact with physical environments effectively.

## why-it-matters
Spatial intelligence is essential for optimizing the user experience in OpenHome. It allows users to intuitively understand space and layout, which is crucial for applications such as home design, augmented reality experiences, and navigating through various environments on the platform. Enhancing spatial intelligence can lead to more effective interactions and better resource allocation within OpenHome.

## how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome platform, spatial intelligence manifests through features that aid in visualizing space, such as interactive maps and immersive virtual environments. Users can leverage these tools to plan and interact with their spaces, understanding dimensions, layouts, and the spatial arrangements of objects. This capability enhances user engagement and facilitates design processes.
