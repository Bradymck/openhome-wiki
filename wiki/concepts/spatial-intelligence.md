## definition
Spatial intelligence is ambient, always-on, context-aware AI that understands what is happening in a room without being explicitly asked — and responds appropriately.

## why-it-matters
The frontier of voice AI is not reactive assistants but proactive room-aware systems. Spatial intelligence is the next paradigm after voice-first.

## how-it-manifests
Abilities that monitor environment (sound, time, presence), infer context, and act without a wake word. The vision for the next generation of OpenHome Abilities.
