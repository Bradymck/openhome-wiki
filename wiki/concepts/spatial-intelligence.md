Spatial intelligence refers to the ability to visualize and manipulate two-dimensional and three-dimensional shapes and spaces, understanding relationships between objects in space.

Spatial intelligence is crucial for enhancing user experience on OpenHome. It allows for intuitive interaction with the platform, as users can engage with spatially organized content, making navigation and information retrieval more efficient.

## how-it-manifests
In OpenHome, spatial intelligence appears in features that utilize spatial layouts and designs, helping users to interact with virtual spaces more effectively.
