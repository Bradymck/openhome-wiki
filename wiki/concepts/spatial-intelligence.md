## definition
Spatial intelligence is the ability to visualize and manipulate spatial relationships within the environment. This includes understanding the positioning of objects, navigating through space, and recognizing patterns in the spatial arrangement of physical entities.

## why-it-matters
In OpenHome, spatial intelligence plays a crucial role in enhancing user experiences by facilitating intuitive interactions with the environment. It allows for more natural navigation of spaces, whether physical or virtual, thereby improving user engagement and satisfaction.

## how-it-manifests
Spatial intelligence manifests in various aspects of the OpenHome platform, particularly in features that involve augmented reality (AR), spatial mapping, and environmental control. For instance, spatially aware applications can suggest optimal furniture arrangements, provide navigation assistance within smart homes, or enhance immersive experiences through AR.
