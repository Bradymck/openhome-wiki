## definition
Spatial intelligence refers to the capability to visualize and manipulate spatial relationships and understand the layout of environments. It is crucial for navigating spaces, solving problems involving physical dimensions, and enhancing interactions in multi-dimensional settings.

## why-it-matters
Spatial intelligence is significant to OpenHome as it enables users to effectively navigate and interact with their surroundings through the platform. By leveraging this type of intelligence, OpenHome can create more intuitive interfaces that enhance user engagement and communication through spatial awareness.

## how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome platform, spatial intelligence manifests through features that involve the layout and design of digital environments, such as virtual home tours, spatial navigation tools, and augmented reality experiences. These features make it easier for users to visualize spaces and interact with their digital homes in a meaningful way.
