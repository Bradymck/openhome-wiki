## definition
Spatial intelligence refers to the ability to visualize and manipulate spatial relationships in physical environments. In the context of OpenHome, it involves creating interactions that allow users to engage with their AI agents using spatial and environmental cues.

## why-it-matters
Spatial intelligence enhances user interactions by making them more intuitive and aligned with how people naturally understand and navigate their surroundings. This capability is essential for creating immersive voice-first experiences that feel seamless and integrated into users' physical spaces.

## how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome platform, spatial intelligence can be observed in the design of AI agents that respond to user positioning, orientation, or movement. For instance, an AI could change its responses based on where a user is located within a room or react differently depending on the spatial context of the conversation.
