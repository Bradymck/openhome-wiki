## definition
Spatial intelligence refers to the ability to understand and interact with the physical environment and spatial relationships within that environment. This includes recognizing patterns, spatial reasoning, and the capacity to visualize objects and their arrangements.

## why-it-matters
In the context of OpenHome, spatial intelligence enhances the user experience by allowing AI agents to navigate and interpret real-world physical spaces. This is particularly relevant for applications that involve smart home devices and interactions within those environments.

## how-it-manifests
Spatial intelligence manifests within the OpenHome platform through the ability of AI agents to manage and control smart home devices based on their physical locations. For instance, an AI might adjust the lighting in a room based on the user's presence or set up a virtual experience that corresponds to the layout of a physical space.
