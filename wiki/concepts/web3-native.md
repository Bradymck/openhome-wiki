## definition
Web3-native means built with blockchain primitives as a first-class concern: token-gated features, NFT identity, on-chain grants, and crypto-native developer incentives.

## why-it-matters
Web3 alignment attracts a specific class of builders who think in terms of ownership, token incentives, and decentralized coordination — the exact mindset OpenHome wants.

## how-it-manifests
The Grant Program has on-chain components. Builders like AquaPrime and deadman.fm integrate wallet authentication and NFT gating directly into Abilities.
