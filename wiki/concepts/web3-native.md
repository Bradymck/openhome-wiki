## definition
Web3 Native refers to applications and platforms that are built specifically for the decentralized web, relying on blockchain technology and distributed systems. These systems promote user ownership, privacy, and transparency.

## why-it-matters
In the context of OpenHome, being Web3 Native empowers users by allowing them to have full control over their data and interactions. This paradigm enhances user trust and fosters a sense of community ownership in the platform's development.

## how-it-manifests
Web3 Native concepts can manifest within OpenHome through features that enable decentralized voice interactions, where users maintain ownership of their voice data and can choose to utilize decentralized abilities for their AI agents. It could also imply integration with blockchain identities or use of decentralized protocols to facilitate interactions.
