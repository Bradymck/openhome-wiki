## definition
Web3-native refers to technologies, applications, and platforms that are built upon decentralized protocols, emphasizing blockchain integration and user sovereignty.

## why-it-matters
Web3-native principles align with OpenHome's mission of creating a decentralized ecosystem where users have control over their voice AI interactions and data privacy. Integrating web3 elements fosters a more open and participatory environment, allowing users to benefit from collective contributions and innovations.

## how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome platform, web3-native aspects could manifest through decentralized identity management, user-controlled data ownership, and community-driven development of voice AI capabilities. By leveraging blockchain technology, OpenHome users can engage with the platform in a way that prioritizes transparency and user governance.
