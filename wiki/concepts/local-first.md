### definition
Local First is a conceptual framework that emphasizes the importance of local resources and data sovereignty, allowing applications to function primarily on local devices while syncing with centralized systems when necessary.

### why-it-matters
This concept is relevant to OpenHome as it prioritizes user control over their own data and interactions, facilitating a more responsive and secure experience. Local-first design enhances accessibility and empowers users, reducing reliance on constant internet connectivity.

### how-it-manifests
In the OpenHome platform, Local First manifests through features that enable applications to operate effectively offline or in low-connectivity environments, ensuring that users can access and manage their data seamlessly regardless of their internet status.
