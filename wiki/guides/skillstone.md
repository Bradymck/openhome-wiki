## who-they-are
Skillstone is a community guide role within the OpenHome ecosystem, aimed at empowering users to develop and utilize voice AI applications effectively.

## what-they-help-with
Skillstone guides specialize in onboarding new users to the OpenHome platform, helping them understand the functionality of Abilities and Personalities, and providing insights on building conversational AI agents. They assist with best practices around using the OpenHome DevKit, local abilities, and integrating hardware with voice AI applications.

## how-to-reach
Connect with Skillstone guides on Discord for assistance and support.
