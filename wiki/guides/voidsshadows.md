### who-they-are
Voidsshadows is a community guide within the OpenHome project, providing insights and assistance to new developers and users in leveraging the OpenHome voice AI platform.

### what-they-help-with
Voidsshadows specializes in guiding users through the development of conversational AI agents known as "Personalities" and adding modular "Abilities" that enhance the capabilities of these agents. They offer support for:
- Building Personalities with custom voices and conversation styles.
- Using the OpenHome API for integration.
- Developing Local Abilities that utilize direct hardware access and Python packages not available in the standard environment.
