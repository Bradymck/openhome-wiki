### who-they-are
The OpenHome guides consist of power users with extensive experience in developing AI-powered voice applications and building conversational AI agents. Many of them have significant industry experience, including full-stack development, and have worked at a production scale with AI technologies such as LLMs (Large Language Models) and RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation).

### what-they-help-with
The guides specialize in assisting developers in creating and deploying conversational AI agents, known as Personalities, using the OpenHome platform. They provide insights on building Abilities—modular voice plugins that can enhance the functionalities of these agents. They also offer troubleshooting support, implementation advice for AI features, and guidance for utilizing developer kits effectively.
