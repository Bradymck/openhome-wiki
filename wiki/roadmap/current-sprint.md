# what-it-is
The current sprint focuses on the development and distribution of OpenHome's V0.1 devkits. These devkits are advanced smart speakers designed to facilitate the creation of AI-powered voice applications and support developers in building conversational AI agents, known as Personalities.

# status
In-progress

# why-it-matters
The initiative aims to empower developers by providing them access to cutting-edge technology. By distributing the V0.1 devkits, OpenHome encourages innovation by allowing developers to prototype and develop new functionalities (Abilities) for the platform.

# eta
Devkits are being shipped globally in small batches, with a timeline for wider availability still being determined.
