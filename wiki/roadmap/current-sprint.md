## what-it-is
The current sprint involves the development and distribution of OpenHome's physical V0.1 devkits. These are advanced smart speakers designed for open-source Voice AI applications, allowing the creation of agents (Personalities) that can be extended with various skills (Abilities).

## status
in-progress

## why-it-matters
This initiative aims to enhance user engagement with the OpenHome platform by providing developers with tangible tools to prototype and build their projects. By incentivizing contributions through the distribution of devkits, OpenHome fosters a collaborative environment for innovation and development within the voice AI space.

## eta
The devkits are being shipped globally in small batches, with details on how to apply available through their dedicated website.
