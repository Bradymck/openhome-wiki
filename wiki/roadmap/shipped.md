## what-it-is
OpenHome has introduced physical V0.1 devkits for developers, which feature cutting-edge smart speakers capable of local processing for music, smart home control, and interaction with AI. Developers can build with the OpenClaw framework or create their own agents.

## status
Shipped

## why-it-matters
The introduction of these devkits enhances the accessibility of OpenHome's voice AI platform, encouraging innovation and community engagement through the development of conversational AI agents, known as Personalities, and various Abilities to enhance their functionality.

## eta
Shipped globally in small batches, with ongoing promotion for devkit recipients.
