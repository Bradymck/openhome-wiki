## what-it-is
OpenHome is an open-source Voice AI platform that allows users to build conversational AI agents called Personalities, which can be enhanced with modular voice plugins known as Abilities. The platform is currently offering physical V0.1 devkits for developers to create and experiment with these agents.

## status
In-progress

## why-it-matters
The OpenHome project aims to enhance human interaction with technology through voice-first applications that are accessible, immersive, and intuitive. By encouraging community contributions and providing tools for development, OpenHome seeks to empower users to create meaningful AI-powered interactions.
