## what-it-is
OpenHome continues to evolve as a voice AI platform, enhancing the capabilities of agents and their interactions through new features and improvements. Current plans include rolling out user-accessible call logs, a polished command line interface (CLI), and expanded abilities to foster community development.

## status
- **In Progress**: User-accessible call logs and CLI development.
- **Shipped**: Physical V0.1 devkits are being distributed to top contributors for feedback and project support.

## why-it-matters
These upgrades aim to improve user experience, enabling developers to better understand interactions and enhancing the functionality of AI agents. The call logs feature will provide insights into usage patterns and performance, while the CLI improvements are essential for a smoother development process. The distribution of devkits supports community engagement and accelerates innovation within the OpenHome ecosystem.

## eta
While exact timelines for upcoming features are not specified, the call logs feature and CLI improvements are expected to be finalized soon, with announcements planned in community channels.
