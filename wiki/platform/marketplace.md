## what-it-is
OpenHome's marketplace is a hub for developers and users to access, create, and share voice AI applications and capabilities. It facilitates the development and deployment of conversational AI agents by connecting users with various resources and tools.

## how-it-works
The OpenHome marketplace allows users to browse and discover community-created Abilities, which are modular voice plugins that enhance the functionality of AI agents called Personalities. Developers can contribute to the marketplace by sharing their own Abilities, while users can explore a wide range of existing options to expand the capabilities of their agents. The marketplace integrates seamlessly with the OpenHome Dashboard, enabling easy management and customization of these AI agents.
