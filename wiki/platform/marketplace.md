### what-it-is
OpenHome Marketplace is an ecosystem integrated within the OpenHome platform that allows developers to browse, share, and contribute abilities for voice AI agents. It serves as a central hub for users to discover modular voice plugins that enhance the capabilities of their AI personalities.

### how-it-works
Within the OpenHome Marketplace, users can explore community-contributed abilities, which are extensions that add specific skills to voice AI agents. Developers can submit their own abilities, fork existing ones, and engage with others to improve and innovate new functionalities. This collaborative approach fosters a diverse collection of tools that users can utilize in their projects.
