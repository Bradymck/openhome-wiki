## what-it-is
The OpenHome platform marketplace is an ecosystem designed to facilitate the buying, selling, and sharing of resources and services related to smart home technology.

## how-it-works
The marketplace operates as a centralized hub where users can connect with various providers of smart home devices, applications, and services. Users can browse listings, read descriptions, and access reviews to make informed purchasing decisions. The platform also supports features like user account management and order tracking.
