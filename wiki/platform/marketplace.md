## what-it-is
The platform marketplace is a digital space where users can discover, purchase, and interact with various applications and services that enhance their experience with the OpenHome ecosystem.

## how-it-works
The marketplace allows users to browse through a collection of available applications and services tailored for OpenHome devices. Users can search for specific functionalities, read descriptions, and view ratings before making a purchase or download. The platform is designed to facilitate a seamless experience in integrating additional capabilities into existing OpenHome setups, ensuring that users can easily access and implement new features to enhance their home automation systems.
