## what-it-is
The OpenHome Marketplace is a centralized hub for sharing and discovering Abilities, which are modular voice plugins that enhance the functionality of AI agents known as Personalities on the OpenHome platform.

## how-it-works
The Marketplace allows developers and users to browse a collection of community-generated Abilities that can be added to their Personalities. Users can search for, fork, or contribute new Abilities, facilitating the expansion of the OpenHome ecosystem. The integration of Abilities into AI agents is made seamless through an intuitive dashboard enabling easy management and deployment.
