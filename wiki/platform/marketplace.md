### what-it-is
The OpenHome marketplace is an integrated platform that allows developers and users to explore, create, and share various voice AI-enabled applications and abilities within the OpenHome ecosystem.

### how-it-works
The marketplace functions as a centralized hub where users can discover community-built abilities—modular voice plugins that enhance the capabilities of their AI agents, called Personalities. Developers can submit their custom abilities, contribute to existing projects, and leverage available resources to create new skills for voice interactions. Users can browse, fork, and implement these abilities into their own Personalities to add features such as API calls, music playback, and home automation.
