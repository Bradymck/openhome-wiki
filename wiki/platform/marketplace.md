### what-it-is
The OpenHome Marketplace is a platform feature that facilitates the sharing and distribution of user-created Abilities and Personalities for OpenHome's voice AI agents. 

### how-it-works
The Marketplace allows developers and users to create, browse, and deploy various voice plugins (Abilities) that can enhance the functionality of Personalities within the OpenHome ecosystem. Users can easily access community-created content, fork existing Abilities, or submit their own for others to use, fostering collaboration and creativity in voice AI development.
