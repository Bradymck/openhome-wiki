### what-it-is
The OpenHome marketplace is a digital platform that facilitates the discovery, purchasing, and management of various integrations and applications compatible with the OpenHome ecosystem.

### how-it-works
Users can browse a wide variety of products and services designed for the OpenHome environment. The marketplace allows developers to publish their applications, making them accessible to users seeking to enhance their OpenHome experience. The platform provides tools for managing purchases, subscriptions, and user permissions related to installed services. Integrations from the marketplace can interact seamlessly with OpenHome devices and services.
