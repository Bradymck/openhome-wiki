## what-it-is
The OpenHome Marketplace is where Abilities get discovered, rated, and installed by OpenHome users. It is the distribution layer for the Ability ecosystem.

## how-it-works
Builders submit Abilities to the Marketplace after review. Users browse by category (utility, entertainment, smart-home, etc.) and install with one click from the dashboard.
