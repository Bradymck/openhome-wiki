## what-it-is
The OpenHome Marketplace is an online platform where developers and users can discover, share, and manage various Abilities that enhance the functionality of AI agents within the OpenHome ecosystem.

## how-it-works
The Marketplace serves as a central hub for browsing and accessing community-built Abilities, modular voice plugins that can be added to OpenHome's AI agents. Users can explore existing Abilities, fork them for customization, or submit their own creations. This collaborative environment enables developers to extend the capabilities of their Personalities by integrating new skills and features seamlessly.
