## what-it-is
OpenHome is an open-source Voice AI platform designed for building conversational AI agents, referred to as Personalities, which can be extended with modular voice plugins called Abilities.

## how-it-works
OpenHome enables users to create Personalities, which are AI agents characterized by a unique voice, persona, and conversation style. Users can enhance their Personalities by adding Abilities—modular plugins that grant new skills such as controlling devices, playing music, and accessing APIs. Abilities are activated through voice interactions using hotwords, which trigger custom Python logic to fulfill user requests. Developers can start by reading documentation, setting up their AI agents, and exploring community-built Abilities through the OpenHome Dashboard and GitHub repository.
