## what-it-is
OpenHome is an open-source Voice AI platform that enables developers to build conversational AI agents, referred to as Personalities, which can be customized and extended with modular voice plugins known as Abilities.

## how-it-works
OpenHome operates by allowing users to create and configure Personalities that possess distinct voices, personas, and conversation styles. Users can enhance these Personalities with Abilities that add various skills such as controlling smart devices, playing music, or running interactive quizzes. Abilities are activated during voice interactions based on specific hotwords, and they also run custom Python logic, providing seamless and interactive user experiences.
