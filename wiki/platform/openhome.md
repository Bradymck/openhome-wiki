## what-it-is
OpenHome is an open-source Voice AI platform designed to enable users to build conversational AI agents called Personalities, which can be enhanced with modular voice plugins known as Abilities.

## how-it-works
OpenHome allows users to create a customized AI agent by providing a unique name, voice, and persona. The core components of the platform include Personalities, which embody the voice AI agents, and Abilities, which are functional plugins that extend the capabilities of these agents. Abilities can perform various tasks such as calling APIs, playing music, or controlling smart home devices. Users can trigger these Abilities through specific hotwords during voice conversations, and they are built with custom Python logic.

The platform supports global shipping of development kits that allow users to prototype and contribute by adding new Abilities. OpenHome fosters a community where users can share ideas, contribute to projects, and access resources through its developer documentation and GitHub repository.
