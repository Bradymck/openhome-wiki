## what-it-is
OpenHome is a collaborative platform designed to enhance home automation and integrated experiences across various devices and services. It aims to provide seamless interactions and connectivity between smart home products.

## how-it-works
OpenHome operates by utilizing a set of protocols and standards that allow different home devices and applications to communicate with one another. This infrastructure enables users to control their smart devices from a unified interface, often through a central hub or dashboard. The platform supports various functionalities, including the integration of voice commands, automation routines, and the ability to add new capabilities through its marketplace. As a result, users can customize their home experience easily, linking devices such as speakers, smart lights, and other IoT hardware.
