### what-it-is
OpenHome is an open-source Voice AI platform that allows users to build conversational AI agents, referred to as Personalities, which can be enhanced with modular plugins known as Abilities.

### how-it-works
OpenHome enables developers to create AI agents by configuring their attributes, such as name and voice, and integrating various Abilities to extend their functionality. Abilities are triggered during voice interactions via hotwords and can execute tasks like calling APIs, playing music, running quizzes, or controlling smart home devices. Users can create and manage these agents through the OpenHome Dashboard and can explore additional community-built Abilities on GitHub.
