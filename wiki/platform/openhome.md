# what-it-is
OpenHome is an open-source Voice AI platform that enables the creation of conversational AI agents, referred to as Personalities, and allows for the integration of modular voice plugins called Abilities, which enhance the functionality of these agents.

# how-it-works
OpenHome functions by allowing users to create Personalities that can interact in conversational ways using voice. Each Personality can be equipped with various Abilities that provide specific skills, such as controlling smart devices, playing music, or responding to inquiries. Users can design these Personalities with unique characteristics and set them up through the OpenHome Dashboard. Abilities are activated through hotwords during voice interactions and can execute custom logic, allowing users to personalize the functionality of their AI agents.
