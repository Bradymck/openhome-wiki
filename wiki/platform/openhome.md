## what-it-is
OpenHome is an open-source Voice AI platform designed to enable users to build conversational AI agents, known as Personalities, and enhance them with modular voice plugins called Abilities.

## how-it-works
OpenHome allows developers to create sophisticated voice-first applications by assembling AI agents that can interact with users through speech. Users can define a Personality, which includes setting a name, voice, and conversational style. Abilities are integrated into Personalities to extend their functionality, providing skills such as playing music, controlling smart devices, or conducting quizzes. These Abilities are activated via voice commands or hotwords during conversations. Developers can also leverage the OpenHome CLI to create and manage these agents efficiently.
