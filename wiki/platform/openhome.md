### what-it-is
OpenHome is an open-source Voice AI platform that enables users to build conversational AI agents called Personalities, which can be extended with modular voice plugins known as Abilities.

### how-it-works
OpenHome operates by allowing developers to create AI agents with unique personas, voices, and conversation styles. Users can enhance these agents by integrating various Abilities—plugins that empower Personalities with additional skills like playing music, controlling smart home devices, or performing interactive quizzes. Abilities are triggered by hotwords during voice interactions and can execute custom logic written in Python. The user-friendly interface through the OpenHome Dashboard streamlines the process of creating and managing agents and their abilities.
