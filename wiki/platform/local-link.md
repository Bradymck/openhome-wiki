### what-it-is
The Local Link is a feature within the OpenHome platform that integrates devices and agents locally, allowing them to communicate and execute tasks without relying on cloud services.

### how-it-works
Local Link allows developers and users to create connections between OpenHome agents and various smart devices within a local network. This integration enables faster response times and increased privacy as interactions are managed locally rather than through external servers.
