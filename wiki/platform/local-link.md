### what-it-is
The Local Link platform is a feature of OpenHome that allows developers to connect their voice AI agents to local hardware and services, enhancing the capabilities of their applications with real-time interactions and control over local devices.

### how-it-works
Local Link enables the integration and interaction of OpenHome AI agents with local resources, such as smart home devices or DIY hardware setups, most commonly through networking technologies. Developers can create abilities that respond to voice commands, triggering actions on local devices in a seamless manner.
