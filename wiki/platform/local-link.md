### what-it-is
Local Link is a feature within the OpenHome platform that allows users to connect their devices for enhanced interaction and control through locally hosted applications and services.

### how-it-works
Local Link enables the integration of various devices within the OpenHome ecosystem, allowing them to communicate and share capabilities directly over a local network. This connection facilitates the running of applications that interact with hardware, such as smart speakers or home automation systems, without relying on constant internet connectivity. Users can create and deploy abilities on devices such as Raspberry Pi, further extending the functionalities of their personal AI agents.
