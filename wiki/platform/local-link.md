## what-it-is
The Local Link is a feature within the OpenHome platform that facilitates local interactions between devices and services without relying solely on cloud connectivity.

## how-it-works
Local Link enables seamless communication among various devices and applications in the OpenHome environment. It allows users to link their smart speakers and other devices to perform tasks, share resources, and enhance the overall functionality of their home automation systems. This ability not only improves response times but also increases reliability by ensuring that critical operations can be executed even without an internet connection.
