### what-it-is
The `platform/local-link` is a feature within the OpenHome ecosystem that facilitates local communication between devices on the same network, enabling seamless interaction and control of local services.

### how-it-works
The `local-link` operates by establishing direct connections between compatible devices, allowing them to exchange data and commands over a local network without requiring cloud-based services. This enhances speed and reliability, as local links minimize latency and provide a stable connection for device interactions. It can be used for functionalities such as streaming media, controlling smart home devices, or sharing user preferences among devices.
