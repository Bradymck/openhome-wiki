### what-it-is
The Local Link is a feature of the OpenHome platform that allows users to connect their voice AI agents with local services and devices, enabling enhanced control and interaction with smart home environments.

### how-it-works
Local Link facilitates the communication between Personalities (voice AI agents) and local systems, such as smart speakers or IoT devices, through modular design. It works by creating a seamless interface that gives users the ability to manage local resources and access functionalities easily.
