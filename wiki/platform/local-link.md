### what-it-is
Platform/local-link is a component of the OpenHome ecosystem designed to facilitate the interaction of the OpenHome Voice AI platform with various local devices and services.

### how-it-works
The local-link enables OpenHome to connect and communicate with devices within the user's local network, allowing for seamless integration of smart home devices, local applications, and other networked services. By leveraging local connectivity, local-link aims to enhance the user experience by providing quicker responses and ensuring that voice interactions with devices do not rely solely on external network connectivity.
