## what-it-is
Local Link is OpenHome's local network discovery protocol that allows speakers and Abilities to communicate with devices on the same network without cloud routing.

## how-it-works
Abilities use Local Link to connect to smart home devices, local APIs, and other network-connected hardware. This is the foundation of the local-first architecture.
