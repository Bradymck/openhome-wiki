## what-it-is
The OpenHome Dashboard is an interactive web-based interface that enables users to create, manage, and customize their AI agents known as Personalities, allowing for the integration of various Abilities and personalization of voice interactions.

## how-it-works
Users access the OpenHome Dashboard to set up their AI agents by defining attributes such as name, voice, and personality traits. Through the dashboard, users can add and configure Abilities—modular voice plugins that enhance the functionality of the agents. The dashboard simplifies the process of managing these capabilities, providing tools for both developers and end-users to optimize their voice AI experiences.
