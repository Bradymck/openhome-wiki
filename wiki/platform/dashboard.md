## what-it-is
The OpenHome Dashboard is a web-based platform that allows users to manage their AI agents, known as Personalities, and configure various settings and abilities for enhanced interaction.

## how-it-works
Users can access the OpenHome Dashboard via a web interface at [app.openhome.com](https://app.openhome.com). This platform provides tools for setting up and customizing the AI agent's persona, voice, and conversation style. Users can easily add modular plugins known as Abilities, which extend the functionalities of their Personalities by introducing new skills, such as playing music, controlling smart devices, and integrating API calls. The dashboard also facilitates volume control and device settings for hardware components, including DevKits, enabling users to tailor the audio experience as needed.
