### what-it-is
The OpenHome Dashboard is a user interface that facilitates the creation and management of AI agents, known as Personalities, and their associated Abilities on the OpenHome platform.

### how-it-works
The Dashboard allows users to create new AI agents by customizing their voice, persona, and capabilities. Users can add modular voice plugins, called Abilities, which enhance the functionality of the agents, enabling them to perform various tasks such as playing music, controlling smart home devices, and responding to queries. The Dashboard serves as the central hub for users to configure their agents, manage Abilities, and access developer resources, including documentation and community contributions.
