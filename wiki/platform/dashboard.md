## what-it-is
The OpenHome Dashboard is a comprehensive web interface designed for managing and controlling OpenHome's physical development kit devices, offering users intuitive access to features and functionalities associated with their voice AI agents.

## how-it-works
The dashboard allows users to onboard their devkits, manage settings, control connected devices, and visualize the performance of their AI agents. Users can access the dashboard via the URL [https://app.openhome.com/dashboard/devkit](https://app.openhome.com/dashboard/devkit). Through this interface, users have the ability to create and customize their AI agents, manage abilities, and monitor interactions, all while leveraging the modularity and flexibility offered by OpenHome's ecosystem.
