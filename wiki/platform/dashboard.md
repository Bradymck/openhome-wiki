## what-it-is
The OpenHome dashboard at app.openhome.com is where users manage their speaker, Personality, installed Abilities, and account settings.

## how-it-works
Accessible at app.openhome.com. Users pair their speaker, browse the Marketplace, install/remove Abilities, and configure their Personality from the dashboard.
