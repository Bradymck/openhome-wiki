## what-it-is
The platform dashboard is a user interface component of the OpenHome ecosystem, designed to provide users with a centralized view and control over their connected devices and services.

## how-it-works
The platform dashboard aggregates information from various connected devices within the OpenHome network, allowing users to manage settings, view statuses, and receive notifications. It typically displays real-time data regarding device performance and system health, enabling seamless interaction between users and their smart home components. Users can access the dashboard through web or mobile applications, providing an intuitive experience for device management and configuration.
