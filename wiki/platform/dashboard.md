## what-it-is
The OpenHome Dashboard is a user interface that allows developers and users to manage and configure their AI agents, known as Personalities, and their associated Abilities.

## how-it-works
The Dashboard provides an intuitive platform for users to create and customize Personalities by setting parameters such as the agent's name, voice, and persona. Users can also browse, create, and manage Abilities, which are modular plugins that enhance the capabilities of Personalities with new skills like playing music, controlling smart devices, and more. Through the dashboard, developers can monitor performance, configure settings, and deploy updates to their AI agents seamlessly.
