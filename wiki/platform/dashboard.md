## what-it-is
The OpenHome Dashboard is a user interface for managing and configuring AI-powered conversational agents known as Personalities. It serves as a central hub for users to create, modify, and deploy various AI capabilities within the OpenHome platform.

## how-it-works
The OpenHome Dashboard allows users to set up their AI agents by configuring their properties, such as name and voice. Users can further enhance their Personalities by adding Abilities, which are modular voice plugins that provide new skills such as controlling smart home devices, playing music, or accessing APIs. Through the dashboard, users can interact with the development environment while monitoring their agents’ performance and capabilities.
