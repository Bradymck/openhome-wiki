## what-it-is
Abilities are modular voice plugins for OpenHome's AI platform, designed to enhance the functionality of conversational AI agents known as Personalities. They enable these agents to perform a variety of tasks by integrating new skills through voice commands.

## how-it-works
Each Ability acts as a plugin that can be triggered during voice interactions by recognizing specific hotwords. When activated, they execute custom Python logic, allowing Personalities to perform actions such as calling APIs, playing music, controlling smart home devices, and running quizzes. Developers can create new Abilities to expand their agents' capabilities, which can then be shared or forked within the OpenHome community.
