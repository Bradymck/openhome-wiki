## what-it-is
Abilities in OpenHome are modular voice plugins designed to extend the functionality of conversational AI agents, referred to as Personalities. Each Ability adds new skills that allow Personalities to interact with users in varied and enriched ways.

## how-it-works
Abilities are triggered by specific hotwords during voice conversations, allowing users to engage with their Personalities. Each Ability is capable of executing custom Python logic and can involve tasks such as calling APIs, playing music, running quizzes, and controlling smart home devices. Developers can create, fork, and submit their own Abilities to enhance the interactive capabilities of Personalities on the OpenHome platform.
