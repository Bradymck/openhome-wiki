## what-it-is
Abilities in OpenHome are modular voice plugins that extend the functionality of conversational AI agents, known as Personalities. Each Ability can add new skills to an agent, enabling it to perform various tasks.

## how-it-works
Abilities are triggered by hotwords during voice conversations and can execute custom Python logic. They allow Personalities to interact with APIs, play music, control smart home devices, conduct quizzes, and more. Developers can create new Abilities and contribute them to the OpenHome ecosystem. The system supports a developer-friendly environment, enabling users to build and customize their interactions with voice AI technology.
