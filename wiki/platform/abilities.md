## what-it-is
Abilities are modular voice plugins within the OpenHome platform that extend the capabilities of conversational AI agents, allowing them to perform a variety of tasks and interact with users in more meaningful ways.

## how-it-works
In the OpenHome ecosystem, each Ability can be thought of as a unique skill that enhances a Personality—an AI agent with its own voice and persona. Abilities can perform various functions such as calling APIs, playing music, running quizzes, or controlling smart home devices. They are activated through specific hotwords during voice interactions. Developers can create custom Abilities using Python logic, making it easy to add new features based on specific user needs or preferences. Users can explore existing Abilities created by the community through the OpenHome marketplace, facilitating collaboration and innovation.
