## what-it-is
Abilities are modular voice plugins in the OpenHome platform that enhance AI conversational agents, known as Personalities, by adding new skills and functionalities.

## how-it-works
In OpenHome, an Ability is a standalone plugin that provides specific capabilities to a Personality, such as calling APIs, playing music, controlling smart devices, or running quizzes. Each Ability can be triggered by hotwords during a voice conversation, and they operate based on custom Python logic. Developers can create and prototype their own Abilities, contributing to a growing library of functionalities for Personalities.
