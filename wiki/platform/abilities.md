## what-it-is
Abilities are modular voice plugins used in the OpenHome platform that extend the functionalities of conversational AI agents, known as Personalities. These plugins provide Personalities with new skills and capabilities to enhance user interaction.

## how-it-works
In OpenHome, each Personality can be enhanced with various Abilities that allow it to perform tasks such as calling APIs, playing music, running quizzes, and controlling smart home devices. Abilities are triggered during voice conversations using specific hotwords, enabling the agent to respond intelligently based on the context of the interaction. Developers can create new Abilities using Python, and they can be shared within the OpenHome community for collaboration and improvement.
