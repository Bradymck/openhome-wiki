## what-it-is
Abilities are modular voice plugins for OpenHome that extend the functionality of conversational AI agents called Personalities, enabling them to perform various tasks and interact with users more effectively.

## how-it-works
Each Ability can be integrated into a Personality, allowing users to customize their conversational AI experience. Abilities are triggered by specific hotwords during voice interactions and run custom Python logic to provide unique skills, such as calling APIs, playing music, controlling smart devices, or answering queries. Developers can create and share their own Abilities, contributing to a growing library that enhances the functionality of OpenHome agents. The platform facilitates ease of use for developers by allowing access to community-built Abilities, which can be forked and adapted for individual projects.
