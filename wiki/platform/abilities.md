### what-it-is
Abilities are modular voice plugins within the OpenHome platform that extend the functionality of AI agents, referred to as Personalities. These plugins enable Personalities to perform specific tasks, such as calling APIs, playing music, or controlling smart home devices.

### how-it-works
In OpenHome, a Personality is an AI agent characterized by its voice, persona, and conversation style. Abilities are integrated into these Personalities and are activated by specific hotwords during voice interactions. Each Ability runs custom Python logic to provide new skills and enhance the user experience. Developers can create and share their own Abilities, fostering a community-driven ecosystem for innovation and collaboration.
