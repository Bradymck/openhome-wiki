## what-it-is
Voice AI refers to the application of artificial intelligence in creating systems that can understand and respond to human speech, facilitating interaction with technology through voice commands.

## how-it-works
Voice AI technology utilizes natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning algorithms to interpret user voice inputs. It typically involves various stages such as speech recognition, intent recognition, and response generation. Users engage with voice AI through smart devices or applications, which interpret spoken commands to perform various tasks, provide information, or control compatible devices within a smart home ecosystem.
