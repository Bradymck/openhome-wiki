## what-it-is
Voice AI is an integral component of the OpenHome platform, enabling the creation and interaction of conversational AI agents, referred to as Personalities. These agents can be extended with modular voice plugins known as Abilities, allowing them to perform various tasks through voice commands.

## how-it-works
Voice AI operates by allowing developers to create Personalities, which are AI agents equipped with a defined voice, persona, and conversational style. Users can enhance these Personalities by adding Abilities—modular plugins that enable specific skills such as calling APIs, playing music, conducting quizzes, or controlling smart home devices. These Abilities are triggered by hotwords during voice interactions, and the underlying systems can execute custom Python logic to support richer user experiences.
