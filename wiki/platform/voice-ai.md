## what-it-is
OpenHome is an open-source Voice AI platform that enables users to create conversational AI agents known as Personalities, which can be augmented with modular voice plugins called Abilities.

## how-it-works
The OpenHome platform allows developers to build and customize Personalities, each equipped with a unique voice and conversational style. Abilities serve as plugins that enhance a Personality's functionality, enabling it to perform various tasks such as calling APIs, playing music, and controlling smart home devices. The interactions with Personalities can be initiated through hotwords during voice conversations, which then trigger specific Abilities that execute custom logic written in Python. This modular approach facilitates the easy extension of capabilities as developers can create new Abilities and integrate them into existing Personalities.
