### what-it-is
Voice AI refers to the technology that enables devices to understand, interpret, and respond to human voice commands. This technology powers various applications, particularly in smart home environments.

### how-it-works
Voice AI systems typically use natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning algorithms to process and analyze voice inputs. When a user speaks a command, the system converts the audio signal into text, interprets the intent behind the command, and then executes the appropriate action or response. This can involve fetching data, controlling smart devices, or providing information.
