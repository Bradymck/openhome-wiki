### what-it-is
Voice AI refers to artificial intelligence technologies that enable devices to interact with users through voice recognition and generation, facilitating tasks through spoken commands.

### how-it-works
Voice AI systems use natural language processing (NLP) to understand and interpret spoken language. They convert voice input into text, analyze the text for intent, and provide appropriate spoken responses or execute commands based on the user's requests. These systems typically involve machine learning models that improve over time as they process more voice data.
