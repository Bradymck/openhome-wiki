## what-it-is
Voice AI is a technology that enables machines to understand and respond to human speech, facilitating interaction between users and devices through natural language processing (NLP).

## how-it-works
Voice AI operates by analyzing spoken language using various algorithms and machine learning techniques. It converts voice input into text, processes the language to determine intent, and generates appropriate spoken responses. This interaction can occur through smart speakers, applications, or integrated voice systems, allowing for seamless communication in various scenarios.
