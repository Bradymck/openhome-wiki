### what-it-is
Voice AI is OpenHome's open-source platform that enables developers to create conversational AI agents, referred to as Personalities, which can engage in natural voice interactions with users.

### how-it-works
Voice AI operates by allowing developers to build Personalities that have unique voices, personas, and conversation styles. These Personalities can be enhanced with Abilities, which are modular voice plugins that provide expanded features such as API interactions, music playback, and smart device control. Abilities are activated through specific hotwords during voice conversations and can incorporate custom Python logic, making the platform highly versatile and adaptable for various use cases.
