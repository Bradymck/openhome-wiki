## what-it-is
A Personality is the AI soul of an OpenHome speaker — a voice agent with a distinct style, name, and behavior that adapts over time to the user. Personalities are not generic assistants; they have character.

## how-it-works
Personalities run locally on the speaker. They interpret speech, route requests to the appropriate Ability, and respond with a consistent voice and persona. Users can swap Personalities.
