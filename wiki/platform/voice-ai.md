## what-it-is
Voice AI is an integral component of the OpenHome platform, designed to facilitate the development of conversational AI agents known as Personalities. These agents are customizable and can be enhanced with various Abilities, allowing for personalized interactions with users.

## how-it-works
Voice AI operates by enabling developers to create conversational agents that possess a voice, persona, and style of conversation. Developers can define these agents using the OpenHome Dashboard, where they can customize their name and capabilities. The inclusion of Abilities, which are voice-activated plugins, allows these agents to perform a variety of tasks such as controlling smart home devices, accessing APIs, playing music, and more. The agents interact with users through voice commands, responding based on the hotwords that trigger the respective Abilities during conversations.
