## what-it-is
The Live Editor is a component of the OpenHome platform that allows developers to create and test conversational AI agents called Personalities, integrating various abilities directly through an interactive interface.

## how-it-works
The Live Editor provides a user-friendly interface for building AI agents, enabling real-time interaction and testing of their functions. It facilitates the arrangement of abilities—modular voice plugins—while allowing developers to view logs and debug their agents. This feature is beneficial for troubleshooting and refining agent performance by simplifying the management of interactions between the AI and the end-user.
