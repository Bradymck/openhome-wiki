### what-it-is
The OpenHome Live Editor is a feature within the OpenHome platform that allows users to create and edit voice AI agents, known as Personalities, and integrate various Abilities, enhancing their functional interactions.

### how-it-works
The Live Editor provides a user-friendly interface for developers to design, test, and iterate on voice AI agents. Users can configure their agent’s attributes, such as name and voice, and add specific Abilities that enable these agents to perform tasks, such as interacting with smart home devices or responding to user inquiries. The Live Editor integrates real-time updating capabilities, allowing developers to see changes as they make modifications. This facilitates rapid prototyping and efficient development of personalized voice AI applications.
