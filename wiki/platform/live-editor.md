## what-it-is
The Live Editor is OpenHome's browser-based IDE for building and testing Abilities in real time against a live speaker or simulator. It is the fastest feedback loop for ability development.

## how-it-works
Access from the dashboard. Write Python, see your Ability respond immediately. No deploy step needed for testing. Submit directly to Marketplace from the Live Editor.
