## what-it-is
The Live Editor is a component of the OpenHome platform that enables developers to create and modify conversational AI agents called Personalities in real-time.

## how-it-works
The Live Editor allows users to build and customize Personalities by configuring their voice, persona, and conversation styles. Developers can add Abilities, which are modular voice plugins that enhance the skills of their Personalities. The editor is designed to provide an intuitive interface for both beginners and experienced developers, facilitating the quick deployment and prototyping of new features. It supports real-time updates, enabling instant feedback and iteration on AI agent designs.
