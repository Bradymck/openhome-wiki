### what-it-is
The Live Editor is a component of the OpenHome platform that allows developers to build and prototype voice applications and abilities dynamically. 

### how-it-works
The Live Editor provides a user-friendly interface where developers can create and edit voice AI abilities in real-time. It allows for the testing and iteration of functionalities that enhance the capabilities of AI agents, known as Personalities. Developers can utilize it to add new skills, modify existing ones, and instantly see the results of their changes, making the development process more efficient and interactive.
