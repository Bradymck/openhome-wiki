### what-it-is
The speakers component of the OpenHome platform refers to the audio output devices that are compatible with the OpenHome architecture. These speakers are designed to integrate seamlessly into home networks, enabling playback of audio content from various sources.

### how-it-works
OpenHome speakers use network protocols to connect to audio sources, allowing users to stream music and other audio content effortlessly. They can interact with other OpenHome components, such as controllers and media servers, to provide a cohesive audio experience throughout a home. The infrastructure supports multi-room audio, enabling synchronized playback across different devices.
