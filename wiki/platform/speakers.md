## what-it-is
The OpenHome physical speaker is the hardware runtime for Personalities and Abilities. It connects to your local network, pairs via the app, and runs AI inference locally or with minimal cloud dependency.

## how-it-works
Speakers are paired via app.openhome.com. Once paired, they download a Personality and any installed Abilities. Abilities run via the CapabilityWorker runtime on the speaker hardware.
