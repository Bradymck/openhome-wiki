### what-it-is
OpenHome speakers are advanced smart devices that enable users to interact with AI through voice. These speakers serve as the foundation for building conversational AI agents, known as Personalities, which can be enhanced with additional modular capabilities.

### how-it-works
OpenHome speakers utilize a range of voice-first technologies, allowing users to engage with AI through natural language. Users can set up their AI agents by creating a Personality with a unique name and voice, then augmenting it with Abilities—plugins that provide enhanced skills such as playing music, managing smart home devices, or performing other tasks based on user commands. The speakers respond to hotwords in conversations, triggering the corresponding Abilities that execute custom logic.
