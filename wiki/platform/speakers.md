### what-it-is
The OpenHome speakers are cutting-edge smart speakers designed to integrate with the OpenHome platform, providing capabilities for music playback, smart home control, and interaction with AI agents.

### how-it-works
OpenHome speakers function by running various modular plugins called Abilities, which allow them to perform different tasks. Users can create a personalized AI agent, known as a Personality, that utilizes these Abilities. The speakers are designed for local operation, meaning they can process commands and perform actions without needing constant internet connectivity. They respond to voice commands and hotwords to engage with users and execute tasks such as accessing music, controlling smart home devices, and interfacing with AI applications.
