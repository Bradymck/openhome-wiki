## what-it-is
The "speakers" component of OpenHome denotes the audio output devices that are integrated within the OpenHome platform, allowing for seamless playback of audio content across various devices in a networked environment.

## how-it-works
Speakers within the OpenHome ecosystem interact with a variety of audio sources and services, facilitating multi-room audio setups and synchronized playback. They utilize a common protocol to connect with other devices, enabling the control of playback features, volume adjustments, and grouping of audio zones.
