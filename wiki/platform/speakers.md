### what-it-is
OpenHome speakers are cutting-edge smart devices that serve as physical interfaces for OpenHome's voice AI platform. These devices allow users to interact with AI agents, referred to as Personalities, that utilize modular voice plugins known as Abilities to perform various tasks.

### how-it-works
The speakers operate through an open-source framework, enabling developers to create and customize AI agents that can respond to voice commands, control smart home devices, play music, and access various information through integrated APIs. Users can create their own Personalities by defining characteristics such as name, voice, and conversational style, while adding Abilities to enhance functionality. The devices are fully operational out of the box, supporting a range of local services without requiring extensive setup.
