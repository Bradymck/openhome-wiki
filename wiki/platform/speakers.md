## what-it-is
Speakers in the OpenHome platform are sophisticated smart devices designed to interact with users through voice commands, enabling a seamless integration of AI-powered functionalities into everyday activities.

## how-it-works
OpenHome speakers operate as part of the broader OpenHome ecosystem, supporting the platform's ability to create personalized Voice AI agents known as Personalities. Users can configure these speakers to respond to voice commands, access music, manage smart home devices, and communicate with AI functionalities via modular plugins called Abilities. Each speaker employs voice recognition technology to interpret commands and manage tasks, while also integrating with APIs and other digital tools to deliver a variety of services.
