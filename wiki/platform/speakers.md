### what-it-is
The Speakers component of the OpenHome platform enables audio playback across various devices within a connected home environment, allowing users to stream music and other audio content seamlessly.

### how-it-works
Speakers in the OpenHome ecosystem communicate over a local network, utilizing a standardized protocol for discovering and connecting to compatible audio sources. Users can control playback, adjust volume, and manage playlists through a common interface, making it easy to switch between speakers or synchronize audio across multiple rooms.
